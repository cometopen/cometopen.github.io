About us
We're a Vietnam-based Tetris tournament-hosting organisation. We've hosted 3 domestic tourneys up to this point, making Comet Open our first appearance on the international scene. Quality means everything to us and we hope you'll have a great time watching or playing in Comet Open. Please refer to our Discord for any criticism or appreciation. As for questions, we'd really appreciate it if you decide to check out our FAQ before hand.
